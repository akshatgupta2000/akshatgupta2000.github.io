About — short thesis summary

I completed an MBA in February 2025 and then began an independent research project in June 2025: Decoding India & US Corporate Landscape — Cross-Sector Fundamentals and Outlook to 2030. The project combines ROCE-driven valuation (DCF & ROCE frameworks), RAPI & CAS macro frameworks, sector scorecards and Excel-based models across 122 companies (India & US) and 11 sectors. The thesis includes detailed company write-ups, sector analysis, and Excel workbooks for reproducible modelling.
