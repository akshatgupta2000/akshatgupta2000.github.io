Key findings (short)

  1. Structural leaders: Defence, Pharma and selected IT/Financial companies show sustained ROCE/earnings mix indicating long-term leadership in respective markets.
  2. Value builders: FMCG shows high ROCE and favorable valuation gaps in India versus US peers — defensive, long-duration cashflow attributes.
  3. Macro sensitivity: Infrastructure and Energy show large CAS (macro) scores — prioritize macro views for tactical exposure.
  4. Valuation gaps: The thesis includes an "Intrinsic vs Market" gap analysis per company — useful for stock selection and sector allocation.
